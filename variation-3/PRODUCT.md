# PRODUCT.md (Variation 3 — Tactical Terminal Mode)

> See `DESIGN.md` for visual tokens and style sheets. This document owns audience, product purpose, brand personality, and design principles.

brand

## Mascot & Ambassador: Milim Nova (Demon Lord of Joy)

In this variation, Milim Nova acts as the **System Administrator / Root Operator** of the Gaia Research mainframe. The site is styled as a secure tactical console. Her messages pop up as high-energy system log alerts and custom admin prompts (`milim@gaia-research:~$`).

## Users

**Primary:** Systems engineers, terminal power-users, and developers who appreciate retro-cybernetic command-line tools. They value raw text, density of information, structural brackets, and keyboard-centric design.

## Product Purpose

The `gaia-research` website acts as a public console to audit and inspect Gaia specifications, safety containment protocols, and open research directories directly from a simulated mainframe.

## Brand Personality

**Voice:** Cyber-Technical / Mainframe Shell — raw terminal outputs, log levels, bracketed metrics, and shell prompt commands, interspersed with Milim's hyper-active root admin notices.

**Tone:** High-density, retro-futuristic, and hacker-grade.

**3-word personality:** Terminal. Mainframe. Glow.

## Design Principles

1. **Monochrome Mainframe:** Build on a pitch-black `#000000` background, using high-contrast terminal greens, cyans, and hot pink alerts for warning triggers.
2. **Brackets as Structure:** Avoid modern boxes and rounded cards. Use brackets `[ ... ]`, dashes `---`, and ASCII lines to separate information and group content.
3. **Flashing Mainframe Feedback:** Use flashing prompts, blinkers, and scanning line overlays to make the terminal feel interactive and alive.
4. **Command-Line Navigation:** Style navigation links as shell tabs or keyboard shortcuts (e.g. `[1] PAPERS`, `[2] TOOLS`).
