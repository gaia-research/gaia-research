# Proof-of-Skill Badges

- **Rank:** 3
- **Viability:** Very High
- **Potential:** High

## What to research
- Dynamic SVG or image badges backed by verifiable capability records.
- Badge states for proposed, active, review, and verified capabilities.
- Optional links from badge to proof page, trace digest, benchmark run, or registry record.

## Why it matters
- Creates visible social proof for developers, repos, demos, and docs.
- Turns registry evidence into distribution and adoption loops.
- Can launch independently of the full Skill Tree while still pushing into it later.
