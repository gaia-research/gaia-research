# North Star Fidelity — Variation 1 Build Contract

**Status:** Draft implementation in progress.

## Intent

Variation 1 rebuilds the existing Next.js homepage as a production, semantic interpretation of the approved North Star Research composition. It is a distinct alternate branch, not a replacement for the Live variation.

The reference composites in `assets/North Star - Gaia Research*.png` define the visual hierarchy, density, section rhythm, and asymmetrical hero topology. They are design references only: no screenshot or rasterized UI may ship.

## Locked direction

- Dense Cyber-Slime Laboratory interface on an obsidian canvas.
- Giant live HTML headline, primary and secondary actions, and a right-side status rail.
- Research Ledger visible directly after the hero, then gateways, directives, and system footer.
- North Star’s distinctive technical framing, restrained pink verification energy, and Rimuru-blue system light.
- Production social surfaces limited to X, Reddit, and GitHub. No Discord CTA/icon.
- Context Diet remains Lab / Benchmark 001.

## Milim posture

Variation 1 uses a static character treatment and remains deliberately easy to iterate. A future pass may explore a non-chibi Milim design or a 3D block sprite; neither is locked or implemented by this branch yet. Any character work must preserve the current persona direction and not imitate third-party IP.

## Definition of done

- The existing app routes remain intact and build successfully.
- The homepage is materially different from the current scaffold and follows the North Star composition in semantic components/CSS.
- Mobile, tablet, desktop, keyboard, reduced-motion, and no-image behavior are deliberate.
- No rasterized UI, fake controls, unverified external imagery, generic dashboard card repetition, or Discord production surface.
- Preview, typecheck, production build, asset-ledger validation, and visual review are recorded in the PR.
