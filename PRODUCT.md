## Mascot & Ambassador: Milim — Chief Capability Scout

The website is steered and hosted by **Milim**, the Chief Capability Scout! She acts as the mascot and open-science ambassador for Gaia Research. The visual style, copy, and interaction patterns represent her high-energy, chaotic-but-brilliant developer energy, backed by the fluid, tactical wisdom of Rimuru.

## Users

**Primary:** AI agent developers, model builders, and open-source contributors looking for cutting-edge verification, safety benchmarks, and capability registries. They are builders who trust hard evidence but want to escape boring corporate documentation.

**Secondary:** Researchers and evaluators auditing capability protocols and contributing new benchmark fusions to the ledger.

**Tertiary:** Tech leads and partners seeking robust, verified agent integrations.

**Emotional goal:** Gosh, it should feel like entering an elite, high-energy research sanctuary where breakthrough science meets absolute joy! Visitors should immediately feel the excitement of pioneering new agent boundaries, wrapped in premium, bold craft that refuses to be boring.

## Product Purpose

Gaia Research is the open research collective behind the Gaia Skill Tree. We define, verify, and push the limits of AI agent capabilities. 

This website serves as the ultimate public portal to:
1. **The Research Ledger:** Peer-reviewed publications, specs, and open-science whitepapers.
2. **Project Gateways:** Quick links to the Skill Tree, Community Bot, and evaluation frameworks.
3. **Mascot Directives:** High-energy updates, safety guidelines, and developer invitations hosted by Milim.

Success: The site becomes the highest-energy reference point in the AI agent ecosystem, proving that rigorous science and delightful design go hand-in-hand!

## Brand Personality

**Voice:** High-Energy Academic — structured scientific frameworks delivered with playful, confident swagger. Milim's voice pops up in hero headlines, calls to action, and section notes (using terms like *Directives, Research Vault, Verification, Fusion, Boss!*), while primary labels and data tables maintain clean, objective precision.

**Tone:** Bubbly, hyper-confident, and meticously crafted. Golly, we're literally the strongest research group, so the data is flawless, and the presentation is spectacular!

**3-word personality:** Joy. Rigor. Spark.

**References:**
- **Solo Leveling / Anime Status Boards:** Clean, technical metrics presented with high-energy rank-ups and bold accents.
- **Nous Research:** A developer-first gateway that cuts straight to code and papers.
- **Premium Streetwear Portals:** Dynamic layout grid structures, contrasting condensed/expanded typography, and bold branding.

## Anti-references

- **Boring Corporate Whitepapers:** Dull gray text, generic blue links, and zero personality.
- **SaaS Cliché Dashboards:** Blue gradients, cookie-cutter icon cards, and dry marketing copy.
- **Low-Contrast Minimalism:** Gray-on-gray text "for elegance" that makes research hard to read.
- **Dry/Stuffy Academic Pages:** Web-native research should be alive, not look like a PDF from 1998.

## Design Principles

1. **Contrast is King (and Queen):** Use massive contrast in font widths (ultra-condensed Bebas Neue paired with wide, expressive Syne) and colors (Milim Pink and Rimuru Blue) to make key messages pop instantly.
2. **Science is Fun:** Data tables, charts, and metrics must be highly dense and accurate, but styled with playful accents, micro-glows, and status lights.
3. **No Saturated Beige:** Skip the generic warm-beige AI default. We build on sleek, obsidian dark canvases that make our pink and blue highlights strike like lightning!
4. **Absolute Transparency:** Every publication and benchmark must link directly to its open-source repository or dataset.
5. **Interactive Playfulness:** Hover states, micro-interactions, and animations should feel alive, snappy, and responsive.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA contrast compliance for all text against backgrounds.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for all layout transitions and status effects.
- Clean semantic markup for screen readers traversing research listings and indexes.
