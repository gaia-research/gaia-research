## Mascot & Ambassador: Milim Nova (Ambassador/Root)

In this variation, **Milim Nova** serves as the **Ambassador & Root Admin** of the Gaia Research platform. She is not a distant corporate spokesperson; she is the core of the system! The copy, structure, and aesthetics reflect her unfiltered, high-impact presence—represented by massive borders, electric neon accents, and zero compromise on layout strength. 

*Milim's Ambassador Directives:*
- "Golly, if your layout isn't screaming with confidence, you're doing it wrong! I'm literally the strongest Demon Lord, so our research grid has to be the sturdiest, thickest, and loudest board on the web!"
- "No soft blurs, no boring gradients, boss! Just raw, high-voltage science and heavy-duty boxes!"

## Users

**Primary:** High-performance developers, autonomous agent engineers, and protocol hackers who value transparency, speed, and raw data presentation. They are tired of over-designed, low-contrast "modern SaaS" layouts and crave tactile, bold information systems that read like terminal hubs or street-art print zines.

## Product Purpose

The `gaia-research` web platform acts as a decentralized-style research dashboard, ledger, and repository for Gaia's sovereign agent benchmarks. It is designed to look like a high-contrast physical grid board, making data nodes and verification statuses look like concrete, unchangeable facts stamped onto a master canvas.

## Brand Personality

**Voice:** High-Energy Neo-Brutalist — raw, direct, and unashamedly loud. Objective, technical research is paired with explosive, high-contrast commentary from Milim Nova, the root ambassador.

**Tone:** Electric, industrial, and hyper-confident.

**3-word personality:** Heavy. Electric. Tactile.

**References:**
- **Neo-Brutalist Web Design (Gumroad, Figma Neo-brutalist trends):** Flat, high-contrast surfaces, solid black drop shadows, 3px solid borders, and intense neon accents.
- **Physical Control Panels & Industrial Interfaces:** Heavy-duty buttons, grid boards, stencil fonts, and warning lights.
- **Developer Zines & Retro Posters:** High-impact text blocks, experimental grids, and bold typography hierarchy.

## Design Principles

1. **Absolute Contrast & Solidity:** Black text and 3px solid black borders rule everything. Every card, table, and button must feel like a physical, heavy-duty object.
2. **Neon Strikes:** Use neon purple (`#d946ef`) and neon slime green (`#22c55e`) exclusively to call out active items, highlight stats, and flag statuses.
3. **No Blurs, No Gradients:** Shadows are solid black offset rectangles. Layout transitions are instant and snappy. 
4. **Structured Grid Rigor:** Everything aligns to a thick, chunky grid. Borders are never hidden; they define the structure.
5. **Bold Typographic Clash:** Force a conversation between wide, geometric Syne display headings and tall, compact Bebas Neue labels, glued together by the high-precision Space Mono for stats.
