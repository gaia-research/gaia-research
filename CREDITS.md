Infinite Skill Craft is a **homage**, not a fork.

Every line of code is original. The *soul* of the mechanic — drag two things together,

## Gaia Skill Tree Contributors 🌳

When a fusion result maps to a **canonical skill** in the Gaia Skill Tree registry,
that skill was authored by a real contributor. Their handle and a deep-link to their
skill page surface in the result card. The recipes that bring those skills to life in
this game are only as good as the contributors who wrote them — thank you for making
the tree grow.

Contributors are credited inline on each canonical result card and at
[gaiaskilltree.com](https://gaiaskilltree.com).
